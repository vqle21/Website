Interests and Extracurriculars

Apart from my research for structural and wind engineering, I am highly interested in digital technologies such as coding, mobile devices, and computer electronics. As corny as it may sound, I am always yearning to expand and add to my toolbox of skills in order to keep up as best as I can with society's technological advancements. This has motivated me in teaching myself the basics of Python and C++ coding, even though my day-to-day uses rely heavily on MATLAB.
