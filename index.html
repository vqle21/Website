Research Highlights

For my doctoral dissertation, my research focus is the development of a computationally efficient means for performance-based assessment of vertical structures (e.g. tall buildings, wind turbines) subjected to wind loads from non-stationary phenomena such as thunderstorm downbursts. The study capitalizes on the implementation of the wavelet-based approaches in simulating output structural responses (e.g. roof displacement, roof acceleration) within a Monte Carlo setting. The stochastic properties of the results are then analyzed utilizing techniques inspired from performance-based engineering.
